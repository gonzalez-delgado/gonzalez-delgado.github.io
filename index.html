Hi!

I am currently an assistant professor in Statistics at ENSAI-CREST. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Simon Gravel group at McGill University. In 2023, I completed my PhD in Applied Mathematics at the Toulouse Institute of Mathematics (IMT) and at LAAS-CNRS, supervised by Pierre Neuvial (IMT) and Juan Cortés (LAAS). My doctoral research was funded by the Labex CIMI.
Here is my CV as of 2025-01.

My research focuses on the development of statistical methods for solving real problems in biology, particularly in structural biology and genetics. Methodologically, I'm interested in selective inference, hypothesis testing, optimal transport and clustering. For more details, take a look at my list of publications.

Javier González-Delgado
Assistant professor at ENSAI-CREST.
